Little bit about me

I am Nawazish Ali, An extremely passionate and enthusiastic self taught web application developer from Karachi, Pakistan. Currently working as a full time employee at a Javascript consultancy firm in Pakistan. I am also running one of the freeCodeCamp’s Local study groups in my city and frequently organize community meetups to help fellow campers and students and provide them with an opportunity to connect with industry professionals. If you don’t already know freeCodeCamp.org is an E-learning platform focused on Javascript, It helped me break into tech industry and I am just trying to give back to the community. I also love doing Open source contributions and you can see some of the projects listed below as well as on my github account.

I have a keen eye for design and you might see that in almost all of my projects. I try my best to make the things I build look beautiful as well.
